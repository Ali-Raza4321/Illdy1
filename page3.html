PURCHASE

Purchasing LAR 01 is the most complete and apparent way to own
an aircraft. It is especially popular among corporate clientele,
service groups and medium-sized companies, and individual
clients. In many countries, such investment can be amortised
through advantageous tax deductions.

LEASING

A form of usage attractive to companies that do not want to or are
not able to purchase a jet. Leasing allows using the LAR 01
in a periodic payment system. After a fixed period, it is possible
to acquire the aircraft and include it into the assets of your
company.

RENTAL

An ideal option for corporate and individual clients who only
occasionally use private aircraft transport. It is possible to pilot the
jet or use the services of professional pilots, depending on the
qualifications of the client. In the latter case, rental resembles an
air taxi service.
